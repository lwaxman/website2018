MediaCity Seoul

creative coding, web, print

In 2016, Math Practice, came up with a generative identity concept for the Mediacity Seoul 2016 Biennale, “Neriri Kiriru Harara,” and invited me to bring their idea to life by writing and implementing a generative collage algorithm for the biennale’s website, posters and other materials.

Graphic design by E Roon Kang and Minsun Eo, at Math Practice.

The script saved collage images each visit, so when navigating between pages you were shown a new coimllage of the same assets.
