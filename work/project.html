Gentex Vehicle Simulator w/ Midnight Commercial

Showing off Gentex technologies in a user-friendly set-up.

user experience, visual design

While I was at Midnight Commercial, we worked with Gentex to help bring their new Vehicle Simulator to life. We designed user scenarios to demonstrate all the technologies they had integrated, and a visual interface for the rear view mirror and heads-up-display that would show users the status of said technlogies. This included an iris-scan personalization scenario that would do things like adjust the seat and mirrors, a home arrival scenario that would open garage doors and turn on lights as the driver approached home, and a toll booth scenario that would automatically pay tolls.
