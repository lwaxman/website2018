Sprout & Kernel

A new visual identity for a nut-based cheese company.

branding, lettering, web design

Sprout & Kernel is an artisinal vegan cheese company based in Australia. I’ve read only good things about their product, but found their identity looked a little out-dated, so as an excercise for myself, I gave them a new visual identity that feels a little more current. I drew the logotype, designed packaging, and mocked up a new website. Photography is pulled from various blogs that’ve featured their products, and their social media.
