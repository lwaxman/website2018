The Patron Experience

Educating customers on Patron tequila in augmented reality.

user experience, visual design, 3d

In collaboration with M Booth, Midnight Commercial created an augmented reality application for Patrón using Apple’s freshly released ARKit. The application brings the Patrón Hacienda to the user’s tabletop, complete with agave field and jimadors, 4 virtual bottles of Patrón tequila and an animated bartender to talk the user through the tasting notes of each expression.

I lead the creation of images for the pitch, and was later responsible for the user experience and design from onboarding to the AR enhanced image capture feature.

For additional information and press, check out Midnight Commercial’s documentation.
