Meindl Figurines

web design, web development

Meindl Figurines is a website dedicated to the life and work of Canadian ceramist and craftperson Elfriede Meindl, in honour of her 90th birthday. The site is seperated by chapters of Elfriede’s life and carreer, with images of her work and subjects she used as inspiration. It's fully responsive, and includes a guest book where people can leave messages or tell stories.

The site uses JQuery for the scroll-animation to skip sections, and a JSON/PHP combo for the guest book.
