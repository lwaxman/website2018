2016 Calendar

illustration, print

A 2016 calendar featuring primarily ficticious animal mixes such as the alpacacorn, octocat and gireel, ending on the duck-bill platypus. Conceptualized, illustrated and printed in medium-blue and flat-gold on a risograph printer, all in a 36 hour frenzy.
