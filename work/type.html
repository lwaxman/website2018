Experimental Type

A series of experiments in interactive web typography.

typography, creative coding, web

In my down time I like to keep to keep up my creative coding skills by playing around at the intersection of design and development with interactive and generative typography. The results are hosted on my Github (find them here) because they are an ever evolving playground more so than a finished product. All experiments (so far) are pure Javascript, without external libraries; in the future I plan to integrate APIs for data-driven interactions.
