Garden

creative coding, illustration, web

A computer drawn flower garden. Pure Javascript, using canvas, no extra libraries. A little offset between the lines and the colour, to give it an ever so slightly less digital feel. Thinking of turning it into an e-card of sorts, or maybe get it to render bouquets rather than scattered plants, and add in some new types of flowers. Not sure. It's a nice visual start, now I just need to find a use for it.
